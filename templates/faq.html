FAQ

How long does my license last?

When you initially purchase your license, you will be able to use the bot for 1 year. You can purchase a 6 month renewal by clicking "Renew" in your dashboard.

How do I change my account password?

If you know your current password, simply click "Dashboard" and then "Change password".

How do I reset my account password?

If you forgot your password, click "Login" then "Forgotten your password?".

How do I change the device my license is activated on?

If you want to move your license to another device, you can do so by either clicking "Log out" in the "Account" section of the bot, or by clicking "Dashboard" and then "Reset device".

What do I do if the bot randomly starts saying I am trying to use multiple devices?

This can happen after certain hardware or software changes and can be fixed by resetting the device your license is activated on. To do this, read the previous answer.
